Unicorn Company Analysis – Project Overview:-

This project focuses on analyzing the global landscape of Unicorn companies—privately held startups valued at over $1 billion. Using Power BI, the analysis highlights key business insights such as valuation trends, country-wise distribution, industry dominance, and investment patterns.

Project Objectives:-
•	To understand the growth and distribution of unicorn companies worldwide.
•	To identify top-performing industries and countries contributing to unicorn creation.
•	To analyze valuation patterns, funding stages, and investor participation.
•	To build an interactive Power BI dashboard for intuitive data exploration.

Key Features of the Analysis:-
•	Global Distribution: Country-wise and region-wise count of unicorn companies.
•	Industry Insights: Breakdown of unicorns by sector (FinTech, AI, E-commerce, EdTech, etc.).
•	Valuation Analysis: Comparison of company valuations and top-valued startups.
•	Funding Patterns: Study of funding rounds, investor participation, and capital raised.
•	Year-wise Trends: Evolution of unicorns over the last decade.
•	Top Investors: Identification of most active venture capital firms.

Dashboard Highlights (Power BI):-
•	Interactive maps showing unicorn distribution globally.
•	Bar/Line charts for industry and valuation trends.
•	Slicers for filtering by year, industry, or country.
•	KPI cards showing total unicorns, average valuation, total funding raised, etc.
•	Drill-down views for detailed company-level information.

Tools & Technologies:-
•	Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)
•	Excel/CSV (Data Preparation)
•	Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)

Outcome:-
This project provides a clear understanding of the unicorn ecosystem, enabling users to explore startup trends, identify key investors, and analyze valuation growth. The Power BI dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions and gain valuable insights into the global startup landscape.
