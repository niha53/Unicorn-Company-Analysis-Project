Unicorn Company Analysis – Project Overview:-

This project focuses on analyzing the global landscape of Unicorn companies—privately held startups valued at over $1 billion. Using Power BI, the analysis highlights key business insights such as valuation trends, country-wise distribution, industry dominance, and investment patterns.

Project Objectives:-
   1.	To understand the growth and distribution of unicorn companies worldwide.
   2. To identify top-performing industries and countries contributing to unicorn creation.
   3. To analyze valuation patterns, funding stages, and investor participation.
   4. To build an interactive Power BI dashboard for intuitive data exploration.

Key Features of the Analysis:-
   1.	Global Distribution: Country-wise and region-wise count of unicorn companies.
   2.	Industry Insights: Breakdown of unicorns by sector (FinTech, AI, E-commerce, EdTech, etc.).
   3.	Valuation Analysis: Comparison of company valuations and top-valued startups.
   4.	Funding Patterns: Study of funding rounds, investor participation, and capital raised.
   5.	Year-wise Trends: Evolution of unicorns over the last decade.
   6.	Top Investors: Identification of most active venture capital firms.

Dashboard Highlights (Power BI):-
  1.	Interactive maps showing unicorn distribution globally.
  2.	Bar/Line charts for industry and valuation trends.
  3.	Slicers for filtering by year, industry, or country.
  4.	KPI cards showing total unicorns, average valuation, total funding raised, etc.
  5.	Drill-down views for detailed company-level information.

Tools & Technologies:-
  1.	Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)
  2.	Excel/CSV (Data Preparation)
  3.	Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)

Outcome:-
This project provides a clear understanding of the unicorn ecosystem, enabling users to explore startup trends, identify key investors, and analyze valuation growth. The Power BI dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions and gain valuable insights into the global startup landscape.
